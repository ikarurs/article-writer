# Analysis Policy

Use the lightest analysis that answers the topic honestly:

- descriptive statistics, simple comparisons, correlations, index construction, and basic time-series plots are preferred;
- regressions are allowed only when the unit of observation, sample size, specification, and limitations are clear;
- do not imply causality from descriptive or associational work;
- keep `analysis/analysis.py` runnable from the package folder;
- write outputs to `outputs/figures/`, `outputs/tables/`, and `outputs/findings.md`.

If the data cannot support an econometric claim, say so in `outputs/findings.md` and keep the result descriptive.
